On the Issues

Student Health

We need to be healthy to learn, that's why Michael supports mental health training for teachers.

School Meals

It's impossible to learn with an empty stomach. Michael will provide free dinners and healthier options to all MOCO students.

Exam Fund

Everyone should be able to pursue their dreams. That's why Michael supports creating a fund to allow all MOCO students to take the AP, SAT, and Magnet exams for free.

Student Passion

The most important thing in learning is being passionate about the subject. Michael will support a shift to project rather than exam based assessments to encourage real understanding.
