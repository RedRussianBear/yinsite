On the Issues

Mental Health

Michael is fighting for mental health training for teachers, who see their students every day and can work with counselors to support students. He's also expanding student-help-student service clubs like the one he founded at Blair, CUPS.

School Meals

It's hard to learn on an empty stomach! Michael will expand MoCo's free dinner program to more schools across the county, while providing healthier and tastier meals that give all students a chance to eat well.

Exam Funds

Everyone should be able to pursue their dreams. That's why Michael supports creating a fund to allow all MCPS students to take the AP, SAT, and Magnet tests for free. After all, no student should have to be held back by test costs.

Student Passion

All too often, school can overload students and make them lose their passions. Michael aims to add back exam week but move toward project-based assessments, which give teachers time to grade and students a chance to review without the stress of RQAs.
